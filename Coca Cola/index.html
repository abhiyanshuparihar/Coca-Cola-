Ingredients

Carbonated water: Provides the fizz and liquid base.   High fructose corn syrup (or sugar): Adds sweetness. Caramel color: Gives Coca-Cola its distinctive brown color.   Phosphoric acid: Adds tartness and acidity. Natural flavors: A blend of various flavoring agents, including coca leaf extract and other undisclosed ingredients.   Caffeine: A stimulant that contributes to the drink's flavor.
